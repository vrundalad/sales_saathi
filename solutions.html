The Optimization Workflow

How data moves through the Sales Saathi architecture to create closed-won revenue.

One-Click Integration

Securely authorize your Salesforce or HubSpot instance via OAuth. Sales Saathi instantly maps your custom fields and begins a historical analysis of your closed-won data to build its baseline intelligence.

Autonomous Data Scraping

Before any scheduled meeting, the engine spiders the prospect's LinkedIn, recent PR announcements, 10-K filings, and job board postings to gather real-world pain points.

Brief Synthesis

Raw data is synthesized into a readable 1-page briefing document. Reps receive this via email or Slack 30 minutes prior to the call, complete with suggested ice-breakers and potential objections.

Live Discovery Coaching

During the Zoom/Teams call, the floating AI widget listens silently. When a competitor is mentioned, a battlecard pops up. When talk-time exceeds 70%, it nudges the rep to ask a question.

Zero-Touch Data Entry

The moment the meeting ends, the transcript is summarized into your exact MEDDIC/BANT fields and pushed directly into your CRM. A follow-up email draft is instantly generated.

Managerial Intelligence

Managers review aggregated dashboard metrics. See which reps are struggling with pricing objections, forecast accurate pipeline close dates, and scale winning strategies across the team.
